Lancaster County Academy - Teacher

2016-17

After graduating from Millersvillle University, I joined Lancaster County Academy. LCA was an alternative school for students at risk of dropping out of high school. I am proud to say that I was part of a staff that gave students dealing with substance abuse, anxiety, bullying, and other unfortunate scenarios a second chance at finishing a high school. While at LCA, I helped students individually with their school work as well as designed and revised curriculum.
